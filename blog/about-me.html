Ko Brooklyn Lyndon-Tonga ahau

Kia ora my name is Brooklyn. I am a descendant of Uepohatu of Ngati Porou and Hineamaru of Ngati Hine. I currently live in the beautiful Whatatutu, Gisborne with my beautiful wildling children and amazing partner, Paora. I lose track of how old i am, ive been 25 for 3 years, maybe 4 now. Im grateful for the opportunity to make myself known and for the opportunity to learn skills i can use to further my career into tech spaces.

I am passionate about:

  • creating a better world for my Tamariki and Mokopuna.
  • learning and growing and sharing my knowledge with others.
  • being a good Tipuna and leaving a positive impact on my Iwi.
  • being there for those who need me.
  • supporting my partner in all they do.
  • supporting my whanau members with everything i have.
  • raising my children to be the best version of themselves.
  • contributing to the well-being of those around me.
  • being a good Kaitiaki of our Whenua and Taiao
