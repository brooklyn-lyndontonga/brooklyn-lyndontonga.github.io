Learning Plan

My long term career goal is to govern my own time while still being able to provide financial support for my whanau.

Dev Academy will help me achieve that by teaching me practical skills that I can use to find remote work in web development.

According to VIA Character analysis these are my top 5 strengths:

1. Gratitude
2. Curiosity
3. Love of learning
4. Prudence
5. Judgement.

Limitations:

Frustration with Non-Conformity:

It can be frustrating for me when others do not follow my preferred methods. This frustration is often internalized, which may lead to stress or dissatisfaction.

Analytical Over-Reliance:

While my emphasis on analyzing facts before making decisions is a strength, it can also lead to "analysis-paralysis," where I may delay decisions while waiting for more information, potentially hindering timely action.

Potential Rigidity:

A strict adherence to procedures and expectations might limit flexibility and adaptability, which can be challenging in dynamic or rapidly changing situations.

3 skills I want to improve:

  • Emotional Intelligence
  • Adaptability
  • Decision Making

This might look like:
1. working on my self-awareness and self-regulation.
2. being flexible and problem-solving.
3. better prioritizing and decisiveness.

Workload Management:

To improve my workload management, I intend to implement and practice these things:

  • Organizational Systems: such as task manager apps like Trello, digital calendar to stay updated with tasks, and have a dedicated space for any work in Google Docs.
  • Managing Deadlines: by setting deadlines for tasks, prioritizing tasks using the Eisenhower Matrix, and conducting regular reviews.
  • Efficient Collaborating: using communication tools such as Slack, scheduling regular check-ins with the team.
  • Tracking Progress: creating a project dashboard to monitor progress, prepare and share progress reports, collect and incorporate feedback from the team.
  • Time Management: Implement time blocking and continue using the Pomodoro method to ensure quality work and good breaks.
  • Review and Adapt: Periodically evaluate my organizational methods and adjust my strategies based on feedback and what is needed.

What facilitators can do to help me succeed:

  • Give clear instructions: Explain course goals, deadlines, and assignments clearly.
  • Offer support: Be available for help and provide useful materials.
  • Engage and motivate: Use interactive teaching methods and be encouraging.
  • Share expertise: Offer expert knowledge and real-world insights.
  • Foster a positive learning environment: Create a respectful and inclusive atmosphere.
  • Assist with problem-solving: Help with technical issues and offer advice.
  • Maintain transparency: Clearly explain grading and any changes to the course and offer honest feedback about my progress.

It is apparent to me that many of the expectations I have of my facilitator are already being met which speaks volumes to the competencies they have. KEEP BEING AWESOME!!!
