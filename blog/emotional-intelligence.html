Emotional Intelligence.

In Sprint 3 we were asked to explore the concepts of EQ and IQ, here are my insights:

06 August 2024.

Aditonal to the brief outline in our handbook, I found the video below a helpful resource to help me understand the importance of Emotional Intelligence in the workplace.

Here are some of the things that stood out to me from this video.

"Being Intelligent about emotions!!"

Daniel Goleman highlights the importance of balancing cognitive intelligence with emotional intelligence to achieve well-rounded success and fulfillment in life.

Knowing Yourself.

  • Being calm and being able to relax when you are upset.
  • Going with the flow and being okay with change.
  • Feeling confident and believing in yourself!!

Controlling Yourself.

  • Understanding your feelings, when you're happy, sad, angry, or stressed.
  • Knowing your strengths and weaknesses.
  • Being resilient and not letting problems get you down for too long.

Understanding Others.

  • Putting yourself in their shoes and being mindful of how others feel.
  • Reading people by understanding what people may be thinking by their body language - reading the room.
  • Helping others by being a support for your friends and whanau.

Getting Along.

  • Inspiring others by motivating people to do their best.
  • Talking things out and communicating clearly and listening to others.
  • Fixing problems by solving disagreements peacefully.
  • Making friends by building good relationships.

I find making friends a neutral space. I would consider myself friendly and will greet people with welcoming politeness, but I find it hard to connect on a deeper level. At times my friendships sometimes feel superficial; being an adult, I find it even harder to make deeper connections with people I consider my friends because of how busy our individual lives are. I am mindful of this and you may notice as I develop in this space that I may share things as a way to relate to my blog readers on a more personal level. However, I am excited to use the new knowledge about emotional intelligence to develop as a great human.
