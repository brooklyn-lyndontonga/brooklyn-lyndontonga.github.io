Te Whare Tapa Wha Wellness Plan:

Practical Applications of Te Whare Tapa Wha:

12 August 2024

Te Whare Tapa Wha is a health model developed by Ta(sir) Mason Durie. It is a health model influenced by Matauranga Maori that focuses on the interconnectedness of physical, mental, spiritual, and social well-being. The model is based on the idea that these four aspects of well-being are interconnected and that imbalances in one area can affect the others.

The four aspects of Te Whare Tapa Wha are:

  • Taha Tinana - Physical well-being
  • Taha Hinengaro - Mental well-being
  • Taha Wairua - Spiritual well-being
  • Taha Whanau - Social well-being

Applying Te Whare Tapa Wha model to Daily Practices:

  • Practice mindfulness

    • Taha Whanau:
